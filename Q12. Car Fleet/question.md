Stack-based fleet merging

Issue Description:
Cars are traveling towards a destination at different speeds.
Determine how many car fleets will arrive at the destination.

This problem uses stack logic to merge monotonic arrival times.

Acceptance Criteria:

Correct number of fleets

Handles same-position edge cases

Time Complexity: O(n log n)

Space Complexity: O(n)
